# Support

If you have any questions, concerns, or issues, we encourage you to seek help through our community support channels.

## How to Get Help

**Discord**

Join our community on Discord to chat with other users and get real-time assistance from our developers and community members. Click the link below to join us: [Join our Discord](https://discord.com/invite/4WvQpVjsn4)

**Telegram**

If you prefer using Telegram, we also have a dedicated group where you can ask questions and get support. Click the link below to join our Telegram group: [Join our Telegram](https://t.me/MadaraStarknet)

**GitHub**

If your problem is mainly related to development issues, please contact us via a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/keep-starknet-strange/madara/issues) or our dedicated [discussions](https://github.com/keep-starknet-strange/madara/discussions).

## About Our Support

Our team and community are here to help with:

- **Installation and Configuration**: Guidance on installing and configuring your Madara client.
- **Troubleshooting**: Assistance with resolving any issues you might encounter.
- **Updates and New Features**: Information on the latest updates and new features.
- **Best Practices**: Tips on best practices for using and maintaining your full node client.
